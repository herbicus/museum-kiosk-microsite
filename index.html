You vs. Natural Selection

A fun data-driven human vs computer competition that teaches some basic concepts of biological diversity, mutation, and natural selection within a museum exhibit-style platform. The competition uses a simple point-based system run through an algorithim that keeps track of a species success (population) or failure (extinction) based on several dramatic enviromental changes.
